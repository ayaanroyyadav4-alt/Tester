METAL MAN

BOSS PROFILE: DWN-009

Metal Man was the first Robot Master built by Dr. Wily specifically for combat. Built with lightweight materials and high-speed gears, he is the master of the circular saw!

Weapon: Metal Blade
Weakness: Quick Boomerang
Stage: The Conveyor Belt Lab

BATTLE STRATEGY

Phase Enemy Action Pro Tip
Movement Jumps based on your shots Don't spam fire! Metal Man only counters when you attack.
Attack Throws 3 Metal Blades Blades travel in straight lines; slide or jump between them.
The Floor Conveyor belts shift direction Watch the arrows on the floor to avoid drifting into him.
