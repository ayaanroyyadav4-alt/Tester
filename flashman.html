Flashman

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

BOSS PROFILE: DWN-014

Flash Man was created to control time itself. By emitting a concentrated burst of light, he can briefly paralyze his surroundings, leaving enemies completely defenseless.

Weapon: Time Stopper
Weakness: Crash Bomb / Metal Blade
Stage: The Crystal Laboratory

BATTLE STRATEGY

Phase Enemy Action Pro Tip
Time Freeze Stops your movement When the screen flashes, you cannot move. Hope you aren't in the path of his rapid-fire shots!
Rapid Fire Fires a burst of pellets He usually fires right after freezing time. Jump frequently to ensure you are mid-air when he shoots.
Terrain Slippery icy floors The floor is uneven and slick. Use small taps on the D-pad to maintain your footing and avoid sliding into him.
