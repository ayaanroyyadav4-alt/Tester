Airman

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

BOSS PROFILE: DWN-010

Air Man is unique because his head is built directly into his torso. He uses a powerful fan to create localized tornadoes and high-velocity winds that can push Mega Man back.

Weapon: Air Shooter
Weakness: Leaf Shield
Stage: The Sky Fortress

BATTLE STRATEGY

Phase Enemy Action Pro Tip
Wind Gust Blows Mega Man away Keep moving forward! If you stop, his fan will push you into the wall.
Attack Tornado Spread He fires several small tornadoes. They have a curved path; try to find the "blind spot" between them.
Movement Leaps to the other side When he jumps, slide or run underneath him to switch sides and continue your assault.
