Crashman

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

BOSS PROFILE: DWN-013

Created by Dr. Wily using parts from both Guts Man and Bomb Man, Crash Man is a demolition expert. He is clad in heavy armor to withstand the blasts of his own Crash Bombs.

Weapon: Crash Bomber
Weakness: Air Shooter
Stage: The Pipe Station

BATTLE STRATEGY

Phase Enemy Action Pro Tip
Reactive Jump Jumps when you fire Crash Man will always jump and counter-attack when you press the fire button. Use this to predict his movement.
Crash Bomb Times explosives His bombs stick to walls and floors before exploding. Don't get cornered by the blast radius.
Agility High-speed climbing He moves quickly across the room; keep your distance and use the Air Shooter's vertical arc to hit him while he jumps.
