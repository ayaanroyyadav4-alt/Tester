Woodman

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

BOSS PROFILE: DWN-016

Made almost entirely of natural hinoki cypress, Wood Man is a unique combat robot. He is heavily armored with a special coating that makes him resistant to many standard attacks.

Weapon: Leaf Shield
Weakness: Atomic Fire
Stage: The Deep Forest

BATTLE STRATEGY

Phase Enemy Action Pro Tip
Defense Activates Leaf Shield Do not fire while the shield is up! Most projectiles will simply bounce off.
Attack 1 Falling Leaves Four leaves drift from the ceiling; stay mobile to find a gap between them.
Attack 2 Shield Launch He throws the shield horizontally; jump over it and counter-attack immediately.
