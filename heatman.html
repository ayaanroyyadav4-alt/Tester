Heatman

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

BOSS PROFILE: DWN-015

Designed like a Zippo lighter, Heat Man can produce temperatures exceeding 12,000 degrees. He is capable of compressing his body into a massive fireball to charge at his enemies.

Weapon: Atomic Fire
Weakness: Bubble Lead
Stage: The Magma Chamber

BATTLE STRATEGY

Phase Enemy Action Pro Tip
Triple Fire Throws 3 fire pillars The pillars grow where they land. Stay in the gaps to avoid taking massive damage.
Charge Becomes a flaming projectile He only charges after being hit. Be ready to jump over him immediately after you land a shot.
Defense High damage resistance Don't waste uncharged shots. Use his weakness to extinguish his flames quickly.
