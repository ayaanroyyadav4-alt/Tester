Quickman

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

BOSS PROFILE: DWN-012

Based on Elec Man's design, Quick Man was built for extreme speed. He is highly agile, though his light armor makes him vulnerable to heavy impacts.

Weapon: Quick Boomerang
Weakness: Time Stopper / Crash Bomb
Stage: The Underground Lab
