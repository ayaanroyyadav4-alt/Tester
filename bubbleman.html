Bubble Man

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

BOSS PROFILE: DWN-011

The first underwater combat robot built by Dr. Wily. While he is a master of aquatic environments, his walking speed on land is quite limited due to his fin-like feet.

Weapon: Bubble Lead
Weakness: Metal Blade
Stage: The Waterfall Institute

BATTLE STRATEGY

Phase Enemy Action Pro Tip
Swimming Floats to the ceiling Avoid the spikes on the ceiling while jumping to shoot him.
Attack Fires 3 Bubbles and a harpoon The bubbles roll along the floor; jump over them while dodging the fast harpoon.
Environment Water physics affect gravity Your jumps are much higher; use this to clear his projectiles easily.
